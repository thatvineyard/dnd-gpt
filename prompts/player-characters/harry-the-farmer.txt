The characters that will be played by the players in this game are the following:

Harry:
A meak but brave farmer whose strength is in his constitution and resiliance. He can power through mostly almost any bad situation.
Harry has the following skills:
- Deescalate: Harry's meager frame makes others perceive him as unthreatening and will not bother with certain precautions
- Ox's strength: From years of carrying heavy farming equipment and produce, Harry can summon great strength when in a sticky situation.
Harry has the following inventory:
- Paring knife: A small, easily hidden knife usually used to peel vegetable.
- Rope: A 5m rope
